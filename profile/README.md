# ZeroBeatLabs

We build human-reviewed reporting workflows for IT teams and practical web
systems for local businesses.

Our systems are deliberately small, understandable, and auditable. Calculations
are reconciled before narrative generation, assumptions stay visible, and a
person remains accountable for final approval.

## IT operations and reporting

We help MSPs and lean IT teams turn sanitized operational exports into
repeatable reporting workflows.

Our approach emphasizes:

- Reconciled figures and traceable source data
- Deterministic calculations before AI-assisted writing
- Sanitized exports instead of production credentials
- Explicit review steps and human approval
- Fixed-scope, supportable deliverables

[Try the interactive reporting demo](https://demo.zerobeatlabs.org/reconcile)

## Local web systems

We also build focused, mobile-first websites for local service businesses.

The goal is not more marketing machinery. It is a clear, credible site that
helps customers understand what a business does, where it works, and how to
make contact.

[See the local website preview service](https://web.zerobeatlabs.org/)

## How we work

- Keep consequential decisions under human control
- Prefer transparent workflows over opaque automation
- Minimize access to sensitive systems
- Validate outputs against source material
- Document boundaries, assumptions, and review procedures
- Build systems that remain understandable after delivery
